Social Media Etiquette for College Students

Do's:

Do audit your privacy settings regularly to control who sees your personal posts
Do assume everything you post is permanent, even if you delete it later
Do use platforms like LinkedIn to highlight your academic projects and connect with industry professionals
Do verify information and news sources before sharing or retweeting them
Do keep your tone respectful when engaging in debates or discussions in public comment sections

Don'ts:

Don't post complaining or negative comments about your university, professors, or peers
Don't share sensitive personal information like your exact location, phone number, or student ID card
Don't use unprofessional profile pictures on accounts linked to your actual name or college email
Don't engage with or feed internet trolls; report harassment instead
Don't rely on a single password across your social and academic accounts
